TECHNOLOGY USED

Analog Watches

Analog watches have quartz mechanism that enables the “ticking” of the hands to indicate the time on the dial. Quartz watches are the most precise and reliable timepieces you can find. They have a mechanical structure that rotates the hands. Analog watches are also known for being more reliable and precise.

Digital Watches

The watch is powered by a low-frequency, microscopic piece of quartz crystal. The quartz crystal receives electricity from the battery via an electronic circuit. In response to the electronic charge, the quartz crystal oscillator vibrates rapidly and precisely (32,768 times/second).

Smart Watches

A smartwatch is a wearable computing device that closely resembles a wristwatch or other time-keeping device. many smartwatches are Bluetooth-capable. The watch becomes a wireless Bluetooth adaptor capable of extending the capabilities of the wearer's smartphone to the watch.
